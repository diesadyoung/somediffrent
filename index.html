Who we are

MITSO International University today is a modern and dynamically developing university that trains specialists in the field of economics, management, law, information technology and linguistics. Today, the university, whose branches were opened in Gomel and Vitebsk more than 25 years ago, has more than 4,600 talented and enterprising girls and boys studying, mastering modern and demanded knowledge in the labor market in the field of law, economics, logistics, management, marketing, information technology and linguistics. The university trains specialists at the first stage of higher education, master's degree, postgraduate studies, retraining and advanced training. Various training courses are widely presented, as well as preparatory courses for centralized testing. Upon graduation, graduates are awarded state-issued diplomas.


Our Mission

To train professionals who are able to preserve and increase the historical, spiritual and cultural values of the Belarusian people, contribute to the development of the state, ensure the unity and solidarity of the trade union movement.
